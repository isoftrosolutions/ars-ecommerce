## 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM AT A GLANCE

### Colors
- **Primary:** `#2563EB` (Blue)
- **Success:** `#10B981` (Green)
- **Warning:** `#F59E0B` (Amber)
- **Danger:** `#EF4444` (Red)
- **Text:** `#111827` (Dark) / `#F1F5F9` (Light)

### Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter (or Poppins)
- **H1:** 32px bold
- **H2:** 24px bold
- **Body:** 14px regular
- **Small:** 12px regular

### Spacing (8px grid)
- Small: 8px
- Medium: 16px
- Large: 24px

### Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: 320px - 640px
- Tablet: 641px - 1024px
- Desktop: 1025px+
